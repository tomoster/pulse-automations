# Playbook 2: Discovery Call

_Trigger: Qualified lead books a call via Calendly._

---

## Before the Call (AUTO)

- Pre-call brief ready (from intake playbook)
- Calendar reminder sent to Tom with brief attached

## During the Call (TOM — 15-30 min)

**Goal:** Understand their pain, not sell. Listen more than talk.

### Questions to Ask
1. "Walk me through a typical day — where do you spend most of your time?"
2. "What's the most annoying repetitive task in your business right now?"
3. "What tools are you currently using?" (CRM, email, spreadsheets, etc.)
4. "Have you tried to automate anything before? What happened?"
5. "If you could wave a magic wand and fix one thing, what would it be?"
6. "What does your team look like? Who's involved in these workflows?"
7. "What's your timeline — is this urgent or exploratory?"
8. "Do you have a budget range in mind?"

### What to Listen For
- Specific time waste (hours/week on manual tasks)
- Tool sprawl (5+ tools that don't talk to each other)
- Frustration with current solutions
- Clear decision-making authority
- Willingness to share access/credentials

### Red Flags
- "We just want to explore AI" (no specific pain)
- Can't articulate what they'd automate
- No budget or wildly unrealistic expectations
- Wants custom AI product, not workflow automation

## After the Call (AUTO)

1. **Tom drops rough notes** (voice memo, bullet points, whatever)
2. **Junior processes:**
   - Summarizes call into structured brief
   - Generates workflow audit: what's manual, estimated time waste, opportunity
   - Maps pain points to service tiers
   - Drafts proposal with recommended tier and pricing
3. **Proposal ready for Tom's review** within 2 hours of call
