# Playbook 1: Lead Intake & Qualification

_Trigger: New lead submits Free Audit form on website, or Tom forwards an inbound inquiry._

---

## Auto Steps (No Tom needed)

1. **Confirmation email** — send immediately on form submission
2. **CRM entry** — save lead data (name, email, company, pain point from form)
3. **Research brief** — Junior analyzes their business:
   - Website scan (what they do, size indicators, tech stack if visible)
   - LinkedIn (company page, Tom's connection degree, employee count)
   - Public info (reviews, social presence, industry)
4. **Pre-call brief** — compile into a 1-page summary for Tom:
   - Company overview
   - Likely pain points (based on industry + form answers)
   - Estimated opportunity size
   - Suggested talking points for discovery call
5. **Calendar link** — include Calendly link in confirmation email

## Tom's Role

- Review pre-call brief before discovery call (2 min)
- If lead is clearly unqualified (too small, wrong industry, tire-kicker), decline gracefully via template email

## Qualification Criteria

- Has a real business with employees or revenue
- Experiencing manual workflow pain they can articulate
- Budget expectation aligns with Quick Win tier minimum ($500+)
- Decision-maker or can get to one quickly
